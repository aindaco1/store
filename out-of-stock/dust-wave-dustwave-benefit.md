<strong>THE FACULTY LOUNGE & FRIENDS</strong> present ...
<br>
<h3>A Dust Wave benefit at Studio 519!</h3>

Join us at <strong>Studio 519 (519 Central Ave NW) on April 17th</strong> for a special night of improv comedy, community, and support for Dust Wave.
<br><br>

<p><strong>The Faculty Lounge</strong> is the premiere improv group in New Mexico -- a dynamic network of exceptionally talented professional actors and improvisers who bring their passion to teaching and performing across the diverse landscapes of New Mexico. For this special benefit, <strong>The Faculty Lounge & Friends</strong> are bringing the laughs for a one-night-only show in support of Dust Wave!</p>
<br><br>

<p>Come ready to enjoy a sharp, playful evening of live performance -- and <strong>bring some extra cash for bidding</strong> during the event. It’s a benefit, after all, so there will be chances to throw in a little more support while you’re there.</p>
<br><br>

<strong>Doors open at 6:15PM, show starts at 7:00PM.</strong>
<br><br>
<strong><i>One night only -- come laugh, bid, and support Dust Wave.</i></strong>
