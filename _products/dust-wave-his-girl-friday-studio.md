## 📰 His Girl Friday 📞

### Screening at The Studio at WESST!

Join us at **The Studio at WESST (609 Broadway Blvd NE) on June 7th at 6:30pm** for a screening of the 1940 screwball comedy classic **_His Girl Friday!_**

Howard Hawks' [*His Girl Friday*](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0032599) crackles with whip-smart dialogue, breakneck pacing, and two of the sharpest performances ever captured on screen from Cary Grant and Rosalind Russell. This is a rapid-fire and relentless film, setting a gold standard for screwball comedy that still feels electrifying today. With its razor wit, newsroom chaos, and romantic sparks flying at 100 miles an hour, *His Girl Friday* is best experienced the way it was meant to be: on the big screen, with a crowd laughing along.

Also, a Dust Wave short film right before! And a merch giveaway too! 🎥 🎉

**Doors open at 6:00pm, show starts at 6:30pm.**

**Only 45 seats available!**

**_See you there!_**
