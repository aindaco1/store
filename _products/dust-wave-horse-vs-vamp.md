## 🐴 Horse vs. Vamp! 🧛

### A Benefit Event for Horseheads + Breakups Suck

Join us at the **Black Cat (3011 Monte Vista Blvd. NE) on May 3rd at 6:30pm** for an evening where we settle the ultimate grudge match between two filmmaker warriors! Featuring:

[**Noah Tucker**](https://www.instagram.com/noahboatproductions), director of the upcoming short film [Horseheads](https://www.instagram.com/horseheadsfilm)

vs.

[**Ben Arndt**](https://www.instagram.com/ben_carndt_), director of the upcoming short film [Breakups Suck!](https://www.instagram.com/breakups_suck_film)

testing their will and resolve in a series of mini-competitions, all in the pursuit of the ultimate prize: **$500** toward the post-production of their films. 🏆

Also, Dust Wave films and other surprises a plenty! 🎥 🎉

**Doors open at 6:00pm, show starts at 6:30pm.**

**_We can't wait to see you there!_**
