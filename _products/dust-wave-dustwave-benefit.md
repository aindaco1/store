**THE FACULTY LOUNGE & FRIENDS** present ...

### A Dust Wave benefit at Studio 519!

Join us at **Studio 519 (519 Central Ave NW) on April 17th** for a special night of improv comedy, community, and support for Dust Wave.

**The Faculty Lounge** is the premiere improv group in New Mexico -- a dynamic network of exceptionally talented professional actors and improvisers who bring their passion to teaching and performing across the diverse landscapes of New Mexico. For this special benefit, **The Faculty Lounge & Friends** are bringing the laughs for a one-night-only show in support of Dust Wave!

Come ready to enjoy a sharp, playful evening of live performance -- and **bring some extra cash for bidding** during the event. It’s a benefit, after all, so there will be chances to throw in a little more support while you’re there.

**Doors open at 6:15PM, show starts at 7:00PM.**

**_One night only -- come laugh, bid, and support Dust Wave._**
